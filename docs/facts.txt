1 A Plaintiff files a complaint includes a Factual Predicate Statement, Jurisdiction & Venue (this particular court), and Legal Basis of Claim
2. The defendant has 2 options
2.1 The Answer (to agree with what is common, to deny what is not true)
2.2 A motion to dismiss: doesn't challenge the facts, it asks the court to dismiss a case that would be considered legally invalid

3. Discovery is the formal process of information gathering between parties, in preparation for trial
includes: interrogatories, requests for documents, and deposition

IT TAKES A LOT OF TIME AND EFFORT TO WRITE REQUESTS AND ANSWER TO REQUESTS

4. Motion for summary judgement

Motion in Limine: motion to request certain evidence be excluded or allowed at trial.

5. Trial (less than 2% go to trial)
