Employment Agreement

This Employment Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 15, 2024, by and between TechCorp Industries Inc. ("Employer") and Jane Smith ("Employee").

1. Position and Duties
The Employee shall serve as Senior Software Engineer. The Employee agrees to perform all duties and responsibilities associated with this position as assigned by the Employer.

2. Compensation
The Employee shall receive an annual base salary of $120,000, payable in accordance with the Employer's standard payroll practices.

3. Benefits
The Employee shall be entitled to participate in all employee benefit plans maintained by the Employer, including health insurance, dental insurance, and 401(k) retirement plan.

4. Term of Employment
This Agreement shall commence on February 1, 2024, and shall continue until terminated by either party with 30 days written notice.

5. Confidentiality
The Employee agrees to maintain the confidentiality of all proprietary information and trade secrets of the Employer during and after the term of employment.

6. Non-Compete
The Employee agrees not to compete with the Employer's business for a period of one year following termination of employment within a 50-mile radius of the Employer's principal place of business.

7. Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the date first written above.

TechCorp Industries Inc.
By: John Doe, CEO

Jane Smith
Employee
